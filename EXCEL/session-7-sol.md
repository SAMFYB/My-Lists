## Gotta Count Em' All

1. Choose 4 distinct groups of 4 people from 16 people
  1. Step-by-step approach: $ \binom{16}{4} \binom{12}{4} \binom{8}{4} \binom{4}{4} $
  1. Permutate and divide approach: $ \frac{16!}{4!4!4!4!} $
